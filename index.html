Felix's Shooting Range

Welcome to Felix's Shooting Range. Our Goal is to get you Drunk!

You have 30 sec to hit all the chickens and Jaegermeister Deers you possible can! But be careful! If you miss a Jaegermeister Deer your amount of Shoots will be increased.

The Default Setting in this Game is set to Normal. But you have the option within the Game on the right side to chagne the settings!

Easy | Medium | Hard

will adapt the Speed of the Game
while

Driver | Bartender | Alcoholic

will increase the amount of Jaegermeister Deers appearing!
